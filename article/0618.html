Understanding the Threats: Scams, Frauds, and Lies

Scams, frauds, and lies in the stock market come in various forms. Common schemes include pump-and-dump tactics, where fraudsters artificially inflate the price of a stock to sell off their shares at a profit, leaving other investors with significant losses. Other scams involve insider trading, Ponzi schemes, and fraudulent financial statements designed to mislead investors.
