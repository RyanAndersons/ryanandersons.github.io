jun 25, 2024 Ryan Anderson

Recently, a big scam using WhatsApp has emerged, tricking people with false stock tips and promises of huge returns. Ryan Anderson, a well-known financial expert, shares tips on how to spot and avoid these scams.

What’s the Deal with the WhatsApp Stock Market Scam?

Scammers are sending stock tips through WhatsApp, claiming you'll get quick and high returns. They even use fake success stories to make you trust them. But once you invest, you usually end up losing money because those returns never happen.

How to Avoid Being Scammed

Do your homework on companies and their leaders. Be cautious if someone promises high returns with no risk. Keep up with the latest market news. Use trusted trading platforms like OzFinTrade. And when in doubt, ask a professional.
